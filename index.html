Who is Jerry Marc Hermoso?

Jerry Marc is a Junior Web Developer with more than 4 years of experience working for SysNet Integrators, Inc. He is an achiever having been a 2-time Alpha awardee of SysNet. The award is given to top employees who display punctuality and effectiveness in work. He enjoys using Node.js as his back-end web language and SQL as his database management, Git for his version control and Windows for his computer OS.

Marc graduated from UST-Angelicum College in 2017 with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. During college, his team earned the Best Thesis Award. He was also a 2-time Dean's Lister.

He is 24 years old and is yearning to learn new and better approaches and technologies that help him do a better job. He can recognize when he's in beyond his level of expertise and be able to ask for help. His definition of success is, "it's the result of hard work, learning from failures and persistence."

But it is not all work for Marc. On his free time, Marc likes to watch movies and TV series, travel with friends, eat his favorite foods like seafood, pasta and pizza. He also plays basketball and video games in PS4 and goes biking on weekends.

WEBFONE

VPMS(Vehicle Parking Management System) - Admin

VPMS is a local web based system for managing parking permit virtually. VPMS-Admin manages parking cards for their tenants and guests and it also have different types of needed reports.

VPMS - Cashier

VPMS-Cashier is a local web based system for the payment of the tenants and guests. Upon tapping the card before going out, it computes the rate of the parking fee based on the amount of time the vehicle was parked. It also prints out receipt.
